About Me

I am currently a research scientist at the State Key Laboratory of CAD & CG, Zhejiang University. Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Utah and Clemson University. I received my Ph.D. degree in 2020 and M.S. degree in 2014 from Xiamen University, and I was a visiting student at the University of New Mexico from 2017 to 2019. My research interests include 3D modeling, physics-based simulation, deep learning, parallel computing, and AR/VR.
